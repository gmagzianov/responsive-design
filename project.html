Why

As the construction industry is evolving, so is the way information is shared and stored.

What

Buildset was designed to help with this issue as a cloud base service specific for the construction industry. The main goal is to have the ability to store, organize, work, and share all types of files and apps that are used in the industry.

Who

The functions of Buildset was created with the users in all of constructuon industry in mind.

What

There are many different types of apps and file sources used in the construction industry and keeping all that organized is hard work. The construction industry looses billions of dollars a year due to mis-communication.

Google Drive

Strengths

Can handle any type of file

It is integrated with gmail

Using Google’s Office applications makes it easy to collaborate with other users on documents, presentations and spreadsheets.

15 gb of free space

Weaknesses

Low download speed as the amount of users gets higher

Weak file sharing security

Opportunities

Help people collaborate in a better and faster process.

Other companies and apps can be integrated into google drive making it more useful for the users.

Threats

A cloud base company can support a specific type of work/files that would take some customers away from google drive.

A stronger security and lower amount of users will help the security and speed of the construction cloud service.

Branding

Buildset is the chosen name of the product as it conveys the idea of a construction product. The name is easy to pronounce and easy to remember which is key in any brand. The name will sound professional in the construction business in spoken and written conversation. I want the brand to be the official construction industry product used around the world. Over time, the familiarity of the product will be the standard with updates keeping up with the construction industry innovations. The users will feel safe using the product as the security will be the highest in the industry. At the moment there is a race on which product is going to be the best or standard in the construction industry and this product will push to be that standard.
