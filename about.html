Upgrade Mode

I decided to go back to school to further my career in construction and went for construction management. After a year in the program, I was hired by a great company that started me off in a position that I was wishing for. I worked hard and started to learn a lot about the business but something started to happen after a year of working. I didn’t feel the fulfilment that I thought I was going to have when starting that path. There was something else that I was doing that brought me joy like no other. I was really getting into videography as it pushed my creative side. I didn’t know if it was normal to have second thoughts about your career and I really didn’t like that I was having those thoughts. I felt that I was choosing to do something that was creative and I really wanted to be surrounded by like minded people. Through the process of creating the company I had to create a website which I found to be such an exciting task.

New Path

There's a certain process of thinking when putting together a video and feeling a similar flow when designing a website. I decided that I would take the path as a creative and put my talent as a video creator into product design. Design shapes how we view the world and so much of the we do things in today’s world was designed by someone that wanted people to have a better life. I want to be part of that and someday be the designer of a product that helps someone in the world to have a better life.
